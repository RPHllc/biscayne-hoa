The Biscayne Point Homeowners Association will hold its **annual meeting** on
**October 15, 2025**, at the **North Beach Youth Center**.

All residents are encouraged to attend. The agenda includes:

- Election of officers
- Review of the annual budget
- Community Q&A

If you cannot attend in person, meeting minutes will be posted afterward.
